🛡️ Network Asteroids

Your firewall core sits at the center of the network.
Rotten packets drift in and threaten your core integrity if they collide.
Shoot them — bigger threats split into smaller ones on impact, just like asteroids.
Use the on-screen buttons: ◀ ▶ rotate, ▲ thrust, ● fire.
Clear each wave to advance. Core integrity hits 0% = game over. ⚡
Press Esc anytime to pause.
